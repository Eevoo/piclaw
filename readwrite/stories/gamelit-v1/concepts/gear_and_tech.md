Gear and Tech — steampunk elements

Core Devices
- Kairo: handheld steampunk talisman that logs quests, reputation, and provides basic HUD reads (stat summaries). Displays as small brass panel with etched glass and a faint rune glow.
- Steam Trams & Ferries: main transport; often require boarding tickets or guild passes.
- Clockwork Pets: small automatons used for companionship, courier tasks, and sometimes sport.

Common Tools
- Pneumatic mail tubes in market districts for small deliveries.
- Mechanical prosthetics and augmentations produced by Clockwrights.
- Arcane batteries: small vials storing ritual energy, regulated by Shrine Orders.

Cultural Impact
- Clockwork technology creates new trades and social friction (augmentation debates).
- Kairo devices are legal identifiers; tampering can be criminal.
- Technology blends with ritual: clockwork shrines, mechanical lanterns, rune-engraved boilers.
