Economy and The Game — how "The Reckoning" works in-world

Currencies and Value
- Shiden: primary coin currency used for trade and contracts.
- Reputation: social currency tracked by Kairo; influences access to guild contracts and discounts.
- Favors: informal currency — owed favors can be called in by guilds and influential NPCs.

The Reckoning: Overview
- Combination of rank, achievements, completed quests, and festival performance.
- Seekers gain "Notches" for significant accomplishments; Notches unlock higher-tier contracts and social privileges.
- Seasonal Reckoning events award title ribbons, material rewards, and rare tech or shrine boons.

Progression and Win Conditions
- Multiple win conditions: become Guildmaster, own and operate a new ferry/bridge, become a Shrine Laureate, or destabilize the Reckoning itself.
- Players choose a long-term goal; the world provides competing incentives and social pressures.
- Losing: public disgrace, loss of Kairo privileges, incarceration, or being blacklisted from guild contracts.

Mechanics for Play
- Contracts: small, medium, large — each has reputation and reward profiles.
- Daily tasks and apprenticeships yield skill progression slowly; exceptional events give Notches.
- Community challenges: guild tournaments, shrine festivals, and engineering fairs.
