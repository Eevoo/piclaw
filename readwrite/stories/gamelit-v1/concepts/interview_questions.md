In-Game Interview Questions — to populate character sheet

Intro: The Guild Registrar taps your Kairo and records your voice.

Basic
1) What is your name, and what do you prefer to be called?
2) What island and neighborhood do you come from?
3) What species are you? Any notable family or clan ties?

Background & Daily Life
4) What is your morning ritual before work or training?
5) What do you do for a living or apprenticeship?
6) Describe a typical day in your life.

Skills & Gear
7) What is your primary skill or trade? How did you learn it?
8) Do you have any clockwork augmentations? If so, describe them.
9) What is the one tool you cannot leave home without?

Goals & Motivations
10) Why did you register as a Seeker? What do you hope to gain from the Reckoning?
11) Who do you want to impress or protect?
12) What would make you quit the game?

Social & Flavor
13) What do you fear most in Amahara?
14) What food or smell would always make you feel at home?
15) Any secrets you keep from your guild?

Optional Roleplay Prompts
- A stranger offers you a caged clockwork nightingale; what do you do?
- A magistrate requests your Kairo for inspection; describe your reaction.
