# Goblins

## Basic Information
**Playable**: Yes
**Average Natural Lifespan**: 40 years

## Physical Description
Short tan to green humanoids with long pointy ears, stubby noses, and sticky saliva.

## Culture & Habits
Goblins are naturally gregarious and often drawn to machines and jewelry. They are happiest in environments where they can be indoors or underground, away from direct exposure to the surface. As long as they have shelter and access to their communities or workshops, goblins are content in most conditions, whether urban, rural, or industrial.

## Gameplay Mechanics
**Sticky Spit**: Useful for adhesion and minor repairs in the field.

**Mechanical Expertise**: Excel at tinkering with any mechanical system—steam engines, industrial metalworks, clockwork devices.

**Size Advantages**: Small stature (3-4 feet tall) allows squeezing through maintenance tunnels and tight spaces.

**Underground Navigation**: Many goblin communities (like Sweetwater Down's) built extensive underground cities, though not all goblins live underground.

**Social Stigma**: Wild goblin populations give civilized goblins a bad reputation in some circles.

## Tech Preferences
Strong affinity for machines and mechanical devices.

## Notable Traits
- Shortest natural lifespan of playable species (40 years)
- Gregarious and social by nature
- Machine enthusiasts
- Attracted to jewelry and decorative items
