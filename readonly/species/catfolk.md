# Catfolk Species Template

## Narrative Description

Catfolk are agile, feline humanoids who move with quiet grace and an aptitude for predatory precision. With sharp ears, whiskers, and eyes that gleam in the dark, they are natural hunters and scouts. Their fur ranges from sleek and spotted to thick and wild, and their tails often betray their mood—twitching with curiosity, flicking in annoyance, or held high in confidence. Though they may appear aloof or secretive, catfolk value loyalty and honor among their kin. They are known for their heightened senses, uncanny reflexes, and a deep connection to the shadows.

Most catfolk live in isolated forests or canyons away from human-dominated civilizations, but many have become cosmopolitan and adaptable, living comfortably anywhere humans can—including cities, towns, and rural areas alike. Their agility and senses make them well-suited for urban life, especially Brassmoor's vertical spaces (rooftops, climbing surfaces), where they excel at navigating heights and tight spaces.

When times are lean and hunting fails, catfolk may abandon sub-adults and younger offspring to survive. This harsh practice stems from a culture that prizes strength, self-reliance, and resourcefulness above all. While they are not without compassion, their survival instincts often override familial bonds. As a result, they are often viewed with suspicion—seen as bullies, thieves, or cutpurses. Though many are uneducated and illiterate, they are far from mindless beasts. Their sharp instincts, quick wits, and natural cunning make them formidable in both combat and deception.

Catfolk are slightly more sensitive to high temperatures and loud sounds than humans. While they can navigate vertical spaces, they are not naturally fond of heights or flying. They prefer environments that are not excessively hot, noisy, or exposed.

Silhouette 1

### Species Starting Bonuses

- Starting Experience: 90 xp
- Starting Skills: Catfolk begin with one rank in Perception. You still cannot train their Perception above rank 2 during character creation.
- Claws: Catfolk possess sharp, retractable claws and may choose to attack with the following weapon profile: (Brawl; Damage +1; Critical 3; Range [Engaged]; Vicious 1).
- Fleet of Paw: Catfolk can perform a second maneuver to move without suffering strain. They still cannot exceed the limitation of two maneuvers in a turn.
- Lightweight Build: Lighter than humans, enabling greater agility but less durability in physical contests.
- Higher Metabolism: Must eat and sleep more frequently than humans due to smaller, faster-burning bodies.
