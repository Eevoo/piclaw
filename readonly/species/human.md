# Human Species Template

## Narrative Description

Humans are average height, average strength, average charm, average lifespan—just average of all the species around. But what they lack in distinguishing attributes, they make up for in collective and individual flexibility.

**Additional Context:**
- Jack of all trades, master of none
- Varied appearance
- Most versatile species
- Most common across all regions
- May or may not be cyborgs (cyborg augmentation is culturally accepted)

## Mechanical Description

### Characteristics

Brawn 2
Agility 2
Intellect 2
Cunning 2
Willpower 2
Presence 2

### Derived Attributes

Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Soak: 2 + Brawn
Silhouette 1

### Species Starting Bonuses

Starting Experience: 110 xp
Starting Skills: Humans begin with one rank in each of two different non-career skills. You still cannot train these skills above rank 2 during character creation.
Ready for Adventure: Once per session as an out-of-turn incidental, a Human may move one Story Point from the Game Master's pool to the players' pool.

## Gameplay Mechanics

- Most versatile species
- Most common across all regions
- Cyborg augmentation is culturally accepted option

## Tech Preferences

Culturally open to technology integration; cybernetic enhancements are widely accepted and normalized.

Source: Realms of Terrinoth, page 60.
