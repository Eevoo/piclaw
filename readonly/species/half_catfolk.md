# Half-Catfolk Species Template

## Narrative Description

Half-Catfolk are the offspring of human and catfolk parents, blending the best traits of both lineages. They appear mostly human in form, but carry distinct feline features: side cat ears that twitch at sounds, a slender tail that betrays mood, and delicate whiskers. Their eyes, with vertical slit pupils, glow faintly in dim light, though they lack the full night vision of their catfolk kin. While they may not move with the same silent grace as full catfolk, they possess a natural alertness and a feline instinct for danger.

Half-Catfolk are often caught between two worlds. Catfolk see them as too human—lacking some instincts and deep connection to nature that define true catfolk. Humans, in turn, see them as too feline—too watchful, too reserved, too different. As a result, most half-catfolk end up leaning more toward human culture, having spent their lives among humans and adopting human ways. They are often pragmatic, observant, and quick to read a room, making them natural diplomats, scouts, or spies. Though they may not fully belong to either world, they are adaptable, resourceful, and often wear their dual nature as a quiet badge of resilience.

Silhouette 1

### Species Starting Bonuses

- Starting Experience: 100 xp
- Starting Skills: Half-Catfolk begin with one rank in Cool. You still cannot train their Cool above rank 2 during character creation.
- Special Abilities: Half-Catfolk choose either the Claws or Fleet of Paw special ability from the Catfolk entry.

Source: Realms of Terrinoth, page 68.
