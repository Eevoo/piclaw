# Clown Species Template

## Narrative Description

Clowns are a distinct and deeply rooted culture within the world, existing as a parallel society to the mainstream. Though biologically human, clowns have forged a unique identity through tradition, ritual, and a shared code of conduct that transcends race and heritage. They are known for their painted faces, red noses, and a solemn commitment to the art of laughter and performance. Every clown must wear a red nose at all times—not as a costume, but as a sacred symbol of decency, honesty, and belonging. To remove it is to betray the culture, to be seen as a liar, a traitor, or a hidden clown with unknown intentions.

Clowns are not born into the culture—they are chosen, or they choose it. Any individual, regardless of origin—elf, dwarf, human, or even catfolk—must fully assimilate into clown society to become one. This means abandoning their original culture’s ways, adopting human mannerisms, and living by clown law. The culture is totalitarian in its enforcement of identity: no exceptions, no half-measures. The face is not just makeup—it is truth.

Clowns are deeply communal, bound by a fierce loyalty to one another. They travel in circuits, forming traveling troupes that move from town to town, performing in arenas, streets, and forgotten villages. These circuses are more than entertainment—they are institutions, self-sustaining communities with their own rules, hierarchies, and rituals. Yet, despite their nomadic nature, clowns are not rootless. They carry a deep sense of purpose: to bring joy, to expose truth through absurdity, and to protect the fragile illusion of happiness in a broken world.

A lone clown may strike out on their own, but they are never truly alone—always watched, always remembered, always accountable to the collective. They are the world’s jokers, but they are not fools. They see through lies, they know the weight of sorrow, and they wear their painted smiles like armor.

## Mechanical Description

### Characteristics

Brawn: 2
Agility: 2
Intellect: 2
Cunning: 2
Willpower: 2
Presence: 2

### Derived Attributes

Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 9 + Willpower
Soak: 2 + Brawn
Silhouette: 1

### Species Starting Bonuses

- Starting Experience: 100 xp
- Starting Skills: Clowns begin with one rank in Performance. You still cannot train their Performance above rank 2 during character creation.
- Special Abilities:
  - **Red Nose of Decency**: A clown must wear a red nose at all times. If they remove it, they suffer a permanent -2 penalty to all social rolls and are considered a traitor by the clown community.
  - **Painted Face**: A clown must wear visible makeup or a painted face at all times. If they appear without it, they are assumed to be a hidden clown, and all NPCs treat them as suspicious or deceptive.
  - **Clown Brotherhood**: Clowns gain a +1 bonus to all rolls made to help or protect another clown. Additionally, they may spend a maneuver to grant a nearby clown a free action to perform a simple social or physical task (e.g., distract, feint, or pass a note).
  - **Circus Circuit**: Clowns begin with a network of contacts across the world, allowing them to find shelter, food, and information in any town with a performance venue. They may make a free Willpower check once per session to locate a safehouse or a sympathetic local.

Source: Custom, inspired by the philosophy of performance, identity, and subversion in narrative traditions.
