# Orc Species Template

## Narrative Description

Orcs are a hardy, pragmatic species shaped by the unforgiving landscapes of the Broken Plains and other harsh regions. Towering and powerfully built, they stand well above most humans, their broad frames and thick limbs built for endurance and strength. Their culture is deeply tribal, rooted in survival, honor, and the strength of the pack. Though often perceived as brutish or savage, orcs are far more complex—practical, observant, and fiercely loyal to their kin. They value directness, honesty, and action over artifice, and their lives are shaped by the need to endure and thrive in brutal environments.

While tribal orcs remain deeply connected to ancestral ways, some have adapted to life among humans and even dwarves, forming small, resilient communities in cities and outposts. These cosmopolitan orcs often serve as guards, laborers, or warriors, using their strength and practical mindset to carve out a place in a world that often fears them. Despite their reputation, orcs are not inherently violent—many are thoughtful, proud, and capable of deep loyalty and honor.

### Species Starting Bonuses

- Starting Experience: 100 xp
- Starting Skills: Orcs begin with one rank in Coercion. You still cannot train their Coercion above rank 2 during character creation.
- Special Abilities: Battle Rage – When making a melee attack, an Orc can choose to add 1 boost to the check to add +2 to the damage dealt by one hit of that attack.

Source: Realms of Terrinoth, page 67.
